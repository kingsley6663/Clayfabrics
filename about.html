About Clayfabrics

Clayfabrics is a premier fashion brand founded by C. ARABA YAWSON, dedicated to bringing you the latest trends in clothing and accessories. Our passion for style and quality drives everything we do.

With years of experience in the fashion industry, C. ARABA YAWSON has curated a collection that blends elegance, comfort, and affordability. We believe that fashion should be accessible to everyone, and our diverse range of products caters to all styles and occasions.

Our Mission

To empower individuals through fashion, helping them express their unique personalities and feel confident in their own skin.

Our Vision

To become the go-to fashion brand for quality, style, and innovation, setting new standards in the industry.
